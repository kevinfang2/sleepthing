About HACKPILLOW:

HackPillow is an application intended for monitoring sleep and providing data through various sensors on iPhones. Users can view the graphed data online and then use the data to make adjustments to their sleep patterns.

In order to use HackPillow, place your phone on top of your pillow after opening the app. Close your eyes and fall into a slumber. The following morning, head to the Head Angle and Disruptions graph on the left menu bar to see how you fared.

HackPillow was made by Kevin Fang and William Park at MakeHacks 2015.
